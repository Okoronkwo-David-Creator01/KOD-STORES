Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days, while express shipping takes 2-3 business days. You'll receive tracking information once your order ships.

Where can I find my order number?

Your order number is included in your confirmation email and is also available in your account dashboard under "My Orders".

Can I change my shipping address?

You can change your shipping address within 1 hour of placing your order. After that, please contact our customer service team for assistance.

What if my package is lost or damaged?

If your package is lost or arrives damaged, please contact us immediately. We'll work with the shipping carrier to resolve the issue and ensure you receive your order.

How do I return an item?

Items can be returned within 30 days of delivery. Visit your account dashboard to initiate a return or contact our customer service team for assistance.

Why isn't my tracking information updating?

Tracking information may take 24-48 hours to appear after your order ships. If you don't see updates after this time, please contact customer service.
